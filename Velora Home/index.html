Lighting Collection Vol. II

The Art of Illumination

Beyond the ordinary — a curated exploration of specialist lighting forms that sculpt atmosphere and transform a house into a sanctuary.

Architectural

Light as Structure

Lighting that becomes architecture itself — embedded, intentional, and inseparable from the space it inhabits.

01
Trough

Linear Trough Lighting

Long recessed channels cut into ceilings or walls project a continuous band of diffused light that defines spatial boundaries with architectural precision.

Coffer

Coffered Ceiling Lights

Lighting integrated inside coffered ceiling panels creates a layered, three-dimensional glow unique to grand interiors.

Grazing

Wall Grazing

Fixtures placed close to textured walls skim light across the surface, dramatically revealing stone, brick, or plaster relief.

Niche

Illuminated Niches

Recessed alcoves lit from within become glowing architectural jewels — ideal for sculptures, vessels, and curated objects.

Valance

Valance Lighting

A horizontal board mounted high on the wall conceals light sources that wash both ceiling and drapery simultaneously.

Soffit

Soffit Downlighting

Lights built into the underside of soffits create clean directional task light in kitchens and corridors.

Backlit

Backlit Onyx Panels

Translucent stone panels — onyx, alabaster, or textured glass — backlit with warm LEDs glow like living tapestries on feature walls.

Column

Column Uplighters

Fixtures set at the base of columns wash upward in dramatic pools, accentuating vertical architectural form.

Sky

Artificial Skylights

LED panels behind frosted glass simulate soft, even natural daylight — ideal for basements and rooms without windows.

Float

Floating Ceiling Bands

A suspended ceiling panel hovers above with concealed lighting — creating the illusion that the ceiling levitates in light.

Decorative Fixtures

Objects of Desire

Statement lighting fixtures that double as sculpture — chosen as much for form as for the light they cast.

02
Heritage

Antique Lanterns

Brass or iron cage lanterns carry old-world warmth into modern hallways and foyers with timeless presence.

Retro-Modern

Sputnik Chandeliers

Radiating arms tipped with globe bulbs — a mid-century icon that injects sculptural energy into dining rooms.

Cluster

Bubble Cluster Lights

Transparent glass spheres clustered at varying heights create an ethereal, cloud-like installation above dining areas.

Natural

Rattan & Wicker Shades

Woven natural fibre shades project intricate shadow patterns across walls and ceilings, bringing warmth and craft.

Geometric

Faceted Metal Pendants

Angular copper or brass geometric shades play with light reflection, casting faceted patterns that animate ceilings.

Statement

Custom Neon Sculptures

Hand-bent neon flex shaped into words or abstract art — the ultimate personalised light sculpture for modern living rooms.

Uplighter

Torchière Floor Lamps

Upward-facing bowl shades reflect ambient light off the ceiling, softening the entire room without a single harsh shadow.

Opulent

Crystal Droplet Pendants

Individual crystal teardrops suspended at staggered heights refract light into a thousand tiny rainbows across ceilings.
